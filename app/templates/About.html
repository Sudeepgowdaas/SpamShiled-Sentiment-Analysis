About Us

Project Name: Spamshield Sentiment Analysis

Spamshield Sentiment Analysis is an innovative solution designed to help content creators by filtering spam and providing sentiment analysis for YouTube video comments. Our goal is to promote community well-being and help enhance the quality of content for audiences.

Target Audience

This tool is specifically crafted for content creators, aiming to provide them with insights into their audience's sentiment while keeping their comment sections spam-free.

Key Features

  • Spam Detection: Automatically filters spam comments, allowing creators to focus on real feedback and interactions.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Classifies comments into positive, neutral, and negative categories, giving insights into viewer opinions.

Technology Stack

We leverage machine learning models to deliver accurate spam detection and sentiment analysis for YouTube comments.

Our Goal

Our mission is to promote community well-being by providing tools that enable content creators to maintain high-quality content and meaningful interactions. This project guides content creators in improving the overall experience for their audience.
